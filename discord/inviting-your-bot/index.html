Inviting Your Bot

Using the example link below, you can invite your bot to any server:

https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=0000000&permissions=00000&scope=bot

You need your bot’s ID and a permission set. To get your bot’s ID, go to your Developer Portal in Discord, click on your bot’s name, then in General Information, copy the bot’s ID. Replace “0000000” that is next to “client_id” with your bot’s ID.

To set proper permission you want your bot to have when joining a server, like moderation permission, you need to use the permission calculator that Discord provides at the bottom of the Bot settings. So set the permission you want and it will provide a number. Use that number and replace “00000” that is next to “permissions”.
