Store

We have a store in which you can buy resources like ram , cpu cores , disk etc using falixcoins.

How to access the store

You would first need to login at https://client.falixnodes.net and then on the side bar you will see a store option, click on it then you will be redirect to the store.

How to use the store

After getting redirected to the store website you will see price of item and a interface to change amount of unit of the item you will like to buy. after selecting the right unit of the item click buy, you should get coins deducted and the item you purchased increased.
