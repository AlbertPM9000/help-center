Uninstalling

If you need to for any reasons like freeing up space, you can uninstall the app.

Windows 10

Right click on the start menu and click “Apps and Features”. Locate FalixNodes and uninstall.

Windows 7/8.1

Open Control Panel and head over to “Programs and Features”. Locate FalixNodes and uninstall.

macOS

Open Finder and click on “Applications” folder found in the sidebar. Locate FalixNodes and drag the app into the trash bin.

Linux

Run:

sudo snap remove --purge falixnodes
