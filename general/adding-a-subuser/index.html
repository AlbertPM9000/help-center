Adding a Subuser

To add a subuser in the control panel, click on the server you want to a user to, then click "Subusers" found in the left sidebar. Click on "Create New" found on the right side of the page. You add a user by using their email address, then click on each checkbox on what you want them to access.

If you need to remove a subuser, click "Revoke" next to their username/email on the subusers list.
