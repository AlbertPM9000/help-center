FalixCoins

What's FalixCoins? "FalixCoins" is our own currency that you can use to exchange into FalixNodes resources. You can earn some FalixCoins by referring friends, keeping your browser open on our website, or go to links.

FalixCoins most of the time will not work on mobile, we recommend you to use a desktop web browser. There are no limits to how many coins you can earn.
